ABOUT NiRC:the Conference


What is NiRC?

McGill's National Integrative Research Conference aims to showcase some of the best interdisciplinary research by undergraduate students across Canada. Join us March 18th for this year's conference.

What is BASiC?

The Bachelor of Arts and Science Integrative Council (BASiC) exists to represent all McGill students enrolled in the Bachelor of Arts and Science (B.A. & Sc.) program, to provide activities and services to enhance the educational, cultural, and social conditions of its members, and to promote their general welfare and interests.
